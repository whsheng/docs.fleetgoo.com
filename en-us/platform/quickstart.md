## Quick Start

FleetGoo is a fleet management SaaS launched by [Mapgoo Technology](https://www.mapgoo.net), supporting multiple fleet and device integrations. It provides comprehensive operational management capabilities including vehicle monitoring, trajectory playback, alarms, and statistical reports. It supports standard device integration and one-stop API integration, suitable for enterprises looking to achieve fleet digitalization quickly.

### Application Scenarios

FleetGoo is suitable for the following fleet management scenarios:

- Government and enterprise vehicles
- Tourism passenger transport
- Logistics and freight trucks
- Car rental
- Engineering vehicles
- Cold chain vehicles
- Sanitation and operational vehicles
- Special vehicles
